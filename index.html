Portfolio

Academic Excellence

98% of RPMS students meet or exceed state academic standards. 92% of students have demonstrated year-over-year improvement in standardized test scores. 87% of students are enrolled in Advanced Placement (AP) or honors courses.

Faculty and Staff

100% of RPMS teachers are certified in their respective subject areas. 95% of teachers engage in professional development programs each year. The school boasts a student-to-teacher ratio of 12:1, ensuring personalized attention for every student.

School culture

97% of students report feeling a sense of belonging and inclusivity at RPMS. 95% of parents are satisfied with the communication and involvement opportunities at the school. RPMS has received a “Gold Medal” award for its positive school climate from the National School Association for two consecutive years

College and Career Readiness

92% of graduating students move on to high school with clear academic or career goals. 80% of 8th-grade students complete career readiness workshops before graduation. 98% of RPMS graduates attend post-secondary education or trade programs.
